## ❓ I don't see my classes. Who should I contact?

If your courses are missing from your schedule on MS Teams, please follow these steps:

1. Reach out to your instructor for that specific course, They might not have created the class yet, or there may be a technical issue.
2. If you're still having issues,
3. Contact a TA or Maurice.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The sooner you report missing classes, the sooner they can be resolved. Don't wait until assignments or lectures have already begun.
