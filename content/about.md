## What is SD Notes?

SD Notes is a supplementary resource for students in the Software Development program

## Why use this?

Textbooks and documentation are often written for people who already know what they are doing. SD Notes is written in plain language, with simple examples, specifically mapped to what is taught in this program. If something from class didn't land the first time, this is a good place to come back to it.

## A reminder

These notes are a supplement. Show up to class, ask questions, and do the work. Use this site when you need a concept explained a different way, or when you want to review something before an assessment.
